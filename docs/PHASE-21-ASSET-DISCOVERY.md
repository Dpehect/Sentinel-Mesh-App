# Phase 21 — Asset Discovery Engine

Adds deterministic enterprise asset discovery and inventory controls.

Included:
- repositories, services, domains, containers, servers, databases and queues
- deterministic asset IDs and fingerprints
- duplicate observation detection
- risk classification
- active, inactive and archived lifecycle
- first-seen and last-seen tracking
- tenant-isolated asset identity
- unit tests
- root verification integration

No cloud API, subscription or paid service is required.
